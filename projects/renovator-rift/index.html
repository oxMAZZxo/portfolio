Renovator Rift

Description Personal Contributions Technical Details Challenges Outcome

Description

Renovator Rift is a 2D platformer set inside a spaceship that has been stranded in space after sustaining significant damage, making it the player's objective to repair it. This was a four-week academic project as part of my Game Studio Module at my university, where students from the Games Development and Games Programming courses formed a single ’Game Studio’.

Personal Contributions

I was selected by the faculty to serve as the Producer for this project. Before it began, I interviewed my fellow students to understand their skills better and to choose my department leads (Design, Art, and Programming). My daily tasks included overseeing the project's progress, ensuring tasks were completed on time, guiding the project to meet the deadline, reviewing newly developed features, and planning the next steps.

Challenges

The main challenge was that I had never led a large group towards a common goal, so this was an ideal opportunity to refine my leadership and project management skills.

Outcome

Although it was a difficult challenge, my team and I came through and submitted our game, earning the best submission in this module from the faculty and achieving a grade of 92%.
