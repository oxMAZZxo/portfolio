Egg Smasher

Unity & C# - Google Play Games Services - Mobile Game Development - Systems Design - Version Control.

I developed Egg Smasher during my study at Ravensbourne University. It took me around 5 weeks to complete the game, which I used as a submission for the Mobile Development mini-module. Initially, I intended to create a fun and casual game, but later on, I decided to integrate Google's Play Games Services into it by using the GPGS Plugin for Unity as well as collaborate with an artist from the Games Development course who helped me figure out the art-style, colour palette, and drew the art as well. The game is available on the Play Store, click the Google Play button below to download!
