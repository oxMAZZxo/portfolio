Egg Smasher

Description Personal Contributions Technical Details Challenges Outcome

Description

A reaction time testing game I developed during my studies at Ravensbourne University. It took approximately five weeks to complete, and I submitted it for the Mobile Development mini-module before publishing it on Google Play. I also collaborated with Amber Bowen, who created all the artwork for the game.

Personal Contributions

Designed and implemented all game systems (mechanics, UI, procedural map, swipe controls, audio) and integrated GPGS (Google Play Game Services) to add achievements and leaderboards to the game, allowing players to compete with each other and stay engaged for longer.

Technical Details

Published on Google Play, learning how publishing platforms sign games. Integrated GPGS to enable the integration of leaderboards, achievements, saved games and events.

Challenges

The primary and sole challenge of this project was integrating GPGS, as it was my first time using a third-party SDK and experimenting with cloud technologies. This challenge helped me to learn and understand cloud technologies better, publish a game, and take a step further into becoming a more experienced programmer.

Outcome

This project allowed me to step into the world of indie game development, learn how to publish games on Google Play and expand my horizons past single-player PC games.
