I’m driven by a deep curiosity for how systems work and how code can bring interactivity to life in meaningful ways. My strengths lie in gameplay programming, tool development, and system architecture, with a strong focus on writing clean, maintainable, and scalable code. I approach programming with a problem-solving mindset, using solid software engineering principles like design patterns, modularity, and performance optimisation—especially important when working across different platforms. Beyond games, I’ve worked with WPF and WinForms, which has broadened my understanding of UI/UX design and application development. Whether I’m building mechanics, designing intuitive tools, or crafting seamless user experiences, I aim to create thoughtful and efficient solutions. I enjoy working in team-based environments where ideas can evolve, and I believe that great games are built not just through code, but through teamwork and iteration. I’m always looking to grow my skills, face new challenges, and contribute to projects that are both innovative and enjoyable to play. If you’re looking for someone who’s not only technically solid but genuinely passionate about game development and software design—I’d love to connect.
