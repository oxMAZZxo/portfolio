Renovator Rift

Unity - Producing - Project Management - Team Planning - Time Management - Version Control - Task Management

This project was part of a four-week mini-module in the second semester of my second year at my university called Games Studio, where the whole class would form one massive studio to collaborate and make one game. My official role in this project was producer (which was decided by the lecturers). In the first phases, I had to interview people to figure out who would be my leads for the tech, art, and design departs, and from there, we interviewed people to see what department they wanted to join/fit in. During the four weeks, my daily tasks included overseeing the project's progress, making sure tasks are completed on time, guiding the direction of the project in order to meet deadline, reviewing newly created features, and planning the next steps.
